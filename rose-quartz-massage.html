Our Philosophy

Healing that
meets you exactly
where you are.

Using myofascial release, Swedish massage, and somatic principles, therapist Kay works with clients to release tension held in the body — whether from daily stress, chronic illness, or lived trauma.

This practice is intentionally designed for neurodivergent individuals and those navigating autoimmune conditions, creating a space where your nervous system is always the priority.
