Everything you need to know before your first or next session with Kay.

New Client Policy

New clients are asked to arrive 10–15 minutes early for a brief orientation, intake form, and to discuss any health concerns with Kay before your session begins.

First Visit
⏰
Arrive On Time

Late arrivals may result in a shortened session to respect the next client's time. We recommend arriving 10 minutes early to relax and transition into your session.

Punctuality
❌
Cancellation Policy

Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations (under 24 hrs) are subject to a 50% session fee. No-shows are charged the full session rate.

Important
📋
Health Intake Form

All new clients complete a health intake form prior to their first session. This helps Kay tailor every session to your body's unique needs and contraindications.

Required
🔄
Rescheduling

Need to reschedule? No problem. Please give us at least 24 hours notice and we'll find a new time that works for you — no fees for rescheduling with proper notice.

Flexible

Drink water before your session, avoid heavy meals 1–2 hours prior, and wear or bring comfortable clothing. Communicate any sensitivities or concerns at the start of your session.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know about booking, sessions, and what to expect.

Is this my first time booking?
Welcome! New clients are encouraged to book the free 30-minute consultation first so Kay can understand your body, health history, and goals before designing your first session.
What should I wear or bring?
Wear comfortable, loose clothing. You'll undress to your comfort level and be properly draped throughout the session. Bring any health records or notes about specific areas of concern.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to secure your preferred time. Popular time slots (evenings and weekends) tend to fill quickly — members get priority booking windows.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before your appointment. Late cancellations are charged 50% of the session fee. No-shows are charged the full session rate. Credits are returned once any applicable fees are processed.
Do you work with chronic illness or trauma?
Yes — this is a core specialty. Kay is trained in somatic and trauma-informed bodywork, and works regularly with clients navigating fibromyalgia, autoimmune conditions, PTSD, and neurodivergence.
Is prenatal massage safe?
Yes, when performed by a trained therapist. Kay uses a specialty table that allows safe face-down positioning. Prenatal massage is available for 2nd and 3rd trimester clients. Always consult your OB before booking.
How do I receive my confirmation?
Once you submit your booking request, you'll receive a confirmation email within 24 hours with your appointment details. A reminder will be sent 24 hours before your session.
Can I request a specific service type?
Absolutely. When booking, select your preferred service. You're also welcome to add notes for Kay — the more she knows about your needs, the better she can prepare for your session.
